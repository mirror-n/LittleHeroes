Our Vision

Character education for children worldwide through AI.

The Reality

Parents are busy and not perfect. Character education is often pushed aside, sometimes even causing conflict. Not every child is lucky enough to have a great mentor. It is an unfair reality where the environment a child is born into determines their starting line.

How It Works

We have reincarnated the greatest figures in history as children's peers and character tutors. Learning honesty from Lincoln and creativity from Da Vinci, children practice wise responses and internalize good character through numerous everyday situations.

Why This Matters

The environment a child is born into should not determine their life. Every child deserves a fair chance to develop good character. We believe 'Little Heroes' can be the social safety net that provides that chance.

Our Promise

We are taking the first step on this great journey. We will continuously evolve and contribute so that more children can grow with 'Little Heroes' and create a better, happier world.
